Calculating the Disintegrations per Minute

You will use the ADM-300 to survey the swipe filter of the package, which will give you the counts per minute (cpm) detected.  In addition to taking into account the net cpm, you will also use the counting efficiency of the survey instrument to calculate the dpm.
