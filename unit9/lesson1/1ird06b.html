Relationship Between Dose and Severity of Effects

When analyzing health effects associated with radiation, you should consider not only the category of effects, but also how the dose received relates to the severity of the biological effects.

The observable biological effects of ionizing radiation can be classified as either deterministic or stochastic based on the dose and response of the biological system to that dose.
