Biological Damage and Forms of Radiation

Some forms of radiation are more capable of causing biological damage than others.  However, to discuss potential biological effects, without consideration of the radiation type, the Roentgen Equivalent Man (REM) concept has been created.  REM is the unit of radiation dose equivalent which takes into account the biological damage from exposure by assigning a Quality Factor to each type of radiation, based largely on its LET.

The REM equation is:

Radiation Absorbed Dose (RAD) x Quality Factor (Q) = Roentgen Equivalent Man (REM).
