Radioactive Materials

Examples of typical sources of radioactive materials in the Air Force include:

  • Compasses or aircraft instruments containing radium.
  • Lenses which contain thorium.
  • Aileron counterweights made from depleted uranium.
  • Magnesium-thorium alloys used in aircraft parts.
  • Electron tubes which contain various isotopes.
  • Radiopharmaceuticals.
  • Instrument radioactive check sources.
