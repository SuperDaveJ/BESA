Specific Locations

The primary locations you should be concerned with when conducting a scatter survey are the:

  • Operator position.
  • Edge of protective wall of operator position.
  • Exposure room door.
  • Walls in area adjacent to the exposure room 30 cm from the surface.
  • Wall behind the chest cassette and the wall adjacent to or behind the panoramic unit for dental x-rays.
  • Film pass-through if applicable.
