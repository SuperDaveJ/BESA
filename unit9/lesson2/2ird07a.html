Medical / Industrial X-Ray Survey Calculation

After conducting the survey, you must determine what levels of annual exposure personnel surrounding the operating x-ray equipment may receive because of their proximity to the equipment.

This is determined by using the data from the scatter survey and the Medical / Industrial X-Ray Survey Calculation to calculate the annual exposure based on the occupancy factor for the exposure.
