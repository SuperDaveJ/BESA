USAFSAM / ESOH Service Center

After notification by the RSO, USAFSAM will immediately provide facsimile instructions for performing an investigation, including any bioassay requirements.  USAFSAM will also request the base RSO to return any dosimeters and/or bioassays in progress at the time of suspected overexposure to USAFSAM for priority processing.

Once processing has been complete, USAFSAM will immediately report the results to the installation RSO by telephone and either facsimile letter or encrypted email.
