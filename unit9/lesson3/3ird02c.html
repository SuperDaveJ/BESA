Purpose of an Overexposure Investigation

Once it’s determined a suspected overexposure has occurred, an overexposure investigation is initiated.

An overexposure investigation is conducted to:

  • Confirm that an overexposure has occurred.
  • Determine the levels of radiation to which personnel were exposed.
  • Identify the need for treatment of associated health effects, if applicable.
  • Attempt to prevent recurrences of overexposures.
