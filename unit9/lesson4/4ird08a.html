Selecting Shielding Based on Type of Ionizing Radiation

Each type of radiation has specific qualities which require various degrees and types of shielding.  In order for shielding to be effective, the materials used must be appropriate for the type of radiation present. 

As always, you should follow the hierarchy of controls when making recommendations.  It is also important to note that controls can be used in combination with other controls to best protect personnel.
