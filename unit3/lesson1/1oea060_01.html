Potential Hazards Within an AO

Potential hazards within an AO can be identified through:

  • Industries in the area.
  • Stained soils.
  • Debris piles.
  • Lagoons.
  • Pits.
  • Ponds.
  • Unnatural topography.
  • Stressed vegetation.
  • Sick animals.
  • Drums.
  • Local resident information.
