Site Identification and Sectoring

The Site Identification and Sectoring step in the OEHSA process defines the area of responsibility (AOR), area of operations (AO), and missions - which include the personnel and equipment executing those missions. Through this step, the delineation and segregation of workplaces takes place.

If Site Identification and Sectoring is occurring in a garrison situation, delineation of the AO may be complete, and association of processes into workplaces would be an administrative exercise.

If sectoring in accordance with counter-chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear, and high-yield explosive zones needs to be completed, you should contact Civil Engineering Emergency Management for the current grid and further guidance. The initial grid breakout is for sampling and analysis and may be sufficient depending on potential threat identification.
