National Center for Medical Intelligence (NCMI)

Medical intelligence is provided through the National Center for Medical Intelligence (NCMI), formerly known as the Armed Forces Medical Intelligence Center (AFMIC).  As part of the DIA, NCMI produces medical intelligence for global force protection and homeland health protection to safeguard U.S. interests worldwide.

In addition to medical intelligence assessments, NCMI produces forecasts and databases on:

    • Foreign military and civilian health care capabilities and trends.
    • Worldwide infectious disease risks.
    • Global environmental health risks.
    • Militarily significant life science issues, including biotechnology and advances in nuclear, biological and chemical medical defense.
