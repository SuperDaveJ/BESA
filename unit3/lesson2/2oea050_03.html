Latrines and Showers

Keep latrines a minimum of 100 yards downwind, downhill, and downstream from the food facility, 100 feet from water sources, and at least 50 feet from living/sleeping areas.

Adequate showers with appropriate soakage pits promote good personal hygiene. Because hand washing is key to disease prevention, you should locate hand washing stations, at a minimum, near latrines, food facilities, and medical facilities.
