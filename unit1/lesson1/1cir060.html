Question Styles

This course uses several different styles of Appraisals and Scenario Challenge Point questions.  Answering all the questions in each lesson will help to ensure that you are learning and retaining the material, which will prepare you for your BESA I Final Exam and for participation in BESA II.

It is important to note that question pages are not linked in the Lesson Map.  You will also not be able to click the Forward button if you are attempting a Scenario Challenge Point.

Select the graphic to view tutorials about the most common types of questions you will see.
