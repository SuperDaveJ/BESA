Completing the Course

Successful completion of the course is recorded when every unit has been completed.  For a unit to be marked as completed, you must pass a Progress Check for the unit with a score of at least 70%.  The Progress Check is another method for checking your understanding, presented as a series of questions in a game-style format.  The Progress Check will become accessible from the Lesson Menu after you have completed each lesson in the unit, and it will remain accessible once unlocked.  You can use the Progress Checks as an opportunity to review the material presented throughout the unit and as preparation for the Final Exam.

Once all units are completed successfully, you will receive a Certificate of Completion for the course, and you may then register to take the proctored Final Exam to receive full credit for upgrade training purposes and attendance at BESA II.  Check with your supervisor to find out how to register for the Final Exam.
