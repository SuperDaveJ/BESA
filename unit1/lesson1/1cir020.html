Course Purpose and Navigation Features

This course is primarily designed for BE enlisted personnel who have attained the 5-skill level.  If that description applies to you, this course will help fulfill your 7-level upgrade training requirement.  Successful completion of this course will be required before you can take the Final Exam.  You must pass the Final Exam prior to enrolling in BESA II, the In-Residence portion of this course.

BESA I can also be used as refresher training for your Readiness Skills Verification (RSV) process or for other ancillary training purposes.  If you’re using the course as ancillary training, after completing this lesson, you will be able to access any unit as needed and are not required to complete the entire course or the Final Exam.

Before you can successfully complete the course, you need to understand how to effectively navigate the pages.  You already know that you need to click the Forward button to view the next screen, but you will miss a great deal of important information if you become too accustomed to simply clicking Forward.  The prompt across the bottom of the page will give you screen-specific instructions as you go through the course.  Go ahead – follow the instructions in the prompt.
