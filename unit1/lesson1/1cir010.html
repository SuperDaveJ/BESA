Lesson Overview

Welcome to the distance learning (DL) portion of the Bioenvironmental Engineering Site Assessment (BESA) course.  This course provides critical site assessment training, applying the Occupational and Environmental Health Site Assessment (OEHSA) concept to both garrison and deployed operations and particularly focusing on Health Risk Assessment (HRA) skill sets.

This course uses a blend of traditional content-focused screens and scenario-based instruction to apply the concepts you are learning to actual situations. Completing the DL course in its entirety will take approximately 17 hours; however, the training is divided into 16 units (including this one) which may be completed during one session or separately.

It is important for you to be aware that this training is the first portion of a larger course.  You will apply the concepts you learn in the DL course during the In-Residence BESA II course.

In this introductory lesson, you will learn about the intended audience for this course and the features that have been built into the training.  The estimated time to complete this lesson is 15 minutes.  Select Forward from the bottom right hand corner of the screen to go to the next page.
