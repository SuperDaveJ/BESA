Narrator: Except for the introductory lesson, the first page of each lesson will look similar to this one.  You’ll be able to read an overview of what to expect and what you will learn in the lesson.  You will also find the estimated time to complete the lesson on the overview page.  Selecting the graphic will allow you to hear instructions about your assignment.

There are many audio elements throughout this course, so be sure to keep your speakers on.  Remember, if you would like to read the audio transcript, you can select it from the Options Menu on the left side of the screen.

Narrator: Notice that the animation shows a person traveling to the second location on the base map, which represents lesson two of this unit.  You can replay the audio and animation by selecting Replay on the graphic or the Repeat button at the bottom of the screen.

The Options menu is one of the features that will help you get the most out of the BESA I course.  Let’s look at some of the other choices you can select from this menu besides the Audio Transcript.

Selecting Help will bring up a list of frequently asked questions (FAQs) about this course.

The Glossary is a helpful reference tool which provides the definitions for many terms and acronyms used in the course.

Selecting Resources will take you to a page with reference materials and useful links related to the unit you are studying.  Clicking the Return button will take you back to the lesson page you were on.

Selecting the Lesson Menu will allow you to choose a different lesson within the unit.

Selecting the Exit button from inside the lesson or from the Lesson Menu allows you to quit, saving your progress.  When you select this button, which is found on the left side of the screen, you will be prompted to verify your decision to quit.  Any lessons you’ve completed or begun will be marked on the Lesson Menu so you can quickly see your progress. 

It is extremely important that you exit the course properly.  Simply closing your browser window will not save your data.
