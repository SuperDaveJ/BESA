Bold Yellow Text

Selecting highlighted text will launch a popup box like this one with more information about the topic.  It is important to select these bold words when you see them, or you may overlook information that is necessary for your understanding of the material.

The popup box uses a graphic background similar to what is shown at the top of the screen, to further emphasize the location of the unit.  Notice that this unit takes place in an urban setting.

You can close the popup box by selecting the X at the top right or clicking anywhere else on the screen outside of the popup box.
