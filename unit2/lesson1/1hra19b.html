Supervise and Review

The culminating phase of HRM addresses supervision and review of the health risk controls that are in place. 

Those responsible for the review process need to determine whether the risks and mission are balanced and should continually evaluate the effectiveness of controls.  Your responsibility is to remain available to assist with adjusting the balances if required.
