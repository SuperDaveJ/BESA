Health Risk Communication

Identifying and analyzing health threats and risks are important to ensure the safety of personnel who may be working in hazardous conditions.  HRA provides a standard method for BE to address health risks and communicate the significance of those health risks to stakeholders.  The concept through which these risks are communicated is known as Health Risk Communication.
