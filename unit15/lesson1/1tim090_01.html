Civil Engineering (CE)

There are several organizations and individuals within CE that may be involved in a TIC/TIM assessment, including:

  • Emergency Management.
  • A Local Emergency Planning Committee (LEPC) representative.
  • The Emergency Planning and Community Right-to-Know Act (EPCRA) point-of-contact (POC).
  • The Fire Department.
  • Pest Management.
  • The GeoBase POC.
  • The Hazardous Waste Program Manager.
  • The Real Property Officer.
