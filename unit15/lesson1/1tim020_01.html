Toxic Industrial Chemicals (TICs)

TICs are chemical compounds used or produced in industrial processes that are toxic to humans and animals or that cause damage to plants.  TICs that are evaluated in the TIC/TIM assessment methodology include toxic gases and highly volatile liquids.  Toxic solids and toxic non-volatile liquids are inventoried but are not evaluated in this methodology. 

Some examples of TICs include ammonia, chlorine, formaldehyde, and sulfuric acid.
