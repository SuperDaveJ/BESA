Specific Data

Your TIC/TIM inventory includes the following:

  • The source and ownership of each TIC/TIM.
  • The quantity (volume, mass, or radioactivity) and type of storage (e.g., one-ton cylinders) of each TIC/TIM.
  • The specific location of each TIC/TIM, including building numbers for on-base TIC/TIM or proximity to the base.  GPS coordinates for the location are also useful when attempting to plot the location and evaluate the risks posed by any TIC/TIM release.
