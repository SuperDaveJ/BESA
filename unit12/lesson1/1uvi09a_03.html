Correct Use and Maintenance of PPE

Once appropriate UV/IR PPE has been selected, it must be used and maintained correctly to protect personnel.  Examples of appropriate use and maintenance of PPE include:

  • Keeping outer clothing free from oils and grease.
  • Ensuring sleeves and collars are kept buttoned to prevent hot metal and sparks from contacting the skin when welding.
  • Wearing caps made from flame-resistant material under welding helmets to prevent head burns.
  • Inspecting eye and face protection equipment prior to each use.
  • Following all manufacturer’s specifications and established guidelines for the PPE.
