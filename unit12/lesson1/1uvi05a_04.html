UVB

UVB is the middle wave type of UV radiation and is the most dangerous form of UV radiation.  This type of Ultraviolet Radiation (UVR) occurs between 280 nm and 315 nm on the electromagnetic spectrum. 

The major source of UVB is the sun; however, other sources include phototherapy and welding.  The primary health concerns from UVB are sunburn, skin aging, skin cancer, and eye damage.
