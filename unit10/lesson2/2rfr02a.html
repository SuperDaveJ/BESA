RFR Emitters and the Air Force

Radio Frequency Radiation (RFR) is a common form of radiation found throughout the Air Force.  RFR emitters are present in both deployed and garrison situations and can be used for radar and electronic countermeasures, communications systems, navigation aids, repair and maintenance purposes, and medical and industrial purposes.   

Typical sources and locations of RFR emitters found in the Air Force vary greatly depending on their application.
