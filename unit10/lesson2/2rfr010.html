Lesson Overview

Because of the prevalence of RFR emitters in both garrison and deployed locations, it is important to be able to identify the emitters as well as the potential health effects they can cause. 

As you and your team work on the HRA, you will:

  • Recall types of RFR emitters.
  • Recall parts and functions of RFR emitters.
  • Recall principles of absorption.
  • Relate Specific Absorption Rate (SAR) to Permissible Exposure Limits (PEL).
  • Recall the potential effects of an RFR exposure.
  • Associate potential biological effects to the dose of RFR.

This lesson should take you approximately 15 minutes to complete.
