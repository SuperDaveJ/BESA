Permissible Exposure Limit (PEL)

The permissible exposure limit (PEL) is the value to which an individual may be exposed without exhibiting damaging biological effects and is based on the emitter’s frequency. The PEL can be determined based on Table 2.1 and Table 2.2 in AFOSH Std 48-9, RFR Safety Program. The commonly used PEL for hazard distance assessments is shown in power density (mW/cm2 or W/m2).
