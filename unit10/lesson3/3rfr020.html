Performing RFR Risk Assessments

In order to protect personnel from exposure to potentially harmful levels of RFR, BE must assess RFR systems for compliance with accepted PELs.  PELs are based on whether the exposure occurs in a controlled environment or an uncontrolled environment.

You will perform an RFR risk assessment whenever you identify an emitter with the potential to produce RFR levels at or above the PEL.  This is done to establish a safe perimeter around the emitter.
