Prevent Probe Burnout

In order to prevent probe burnout, the probe should be better than the meter.  For instance, if the probe can handle intensity far above the full scale value of the meter, you are relatively safe from probe burnout. However, the meter does not protect the probe.  You protect the probe by not allowing the meter to peg-out.  Not allowing your meter to peg is a standard practice when using any type of measuring equipment.
