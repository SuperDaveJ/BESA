Estimated Hazard Distance (Dpel)

The estimated hazard distance is the distance from the antenna to the point where the power density equals the PEL.  This is used to determine a safe starting point when conducting a survey.  The Dpel must be calculated before attempting any field measurements.

It’s important to recognize that the measured Dpel will likely be different than the estimated Dpel.
