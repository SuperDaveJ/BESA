Preparing the Investigation Report

To conclude the investigation, a complete and detailed report must be prepared.  The report should include who was involved, what specifically occurred, when it happened, and where the incident occurred, as well as photographs, measured data, and any medical findings that were collected.

The investigator should prepare the final report and submit it to Public Health (PH) within 45 days following its completion.
