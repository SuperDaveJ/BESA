Perform an Initial Estimation of the Exposure

While performing an initial estimation of the exposure, you should gather all information currently known about the incident.  This would include considering the type of emitter and the potential for the individual to be exposed to RFR above the PEL.

When determining if a person has received an exposure in excess of the PEL, you need to consider the average exposure time and whole-body spatial averaging that could be associated with the incident.
