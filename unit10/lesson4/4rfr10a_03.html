Exposed Within Near-Field

If the individual was exposed within the near-field range of the emitter, you should calculate the power density using the power density near-field calculation (Snf).  Before this can be done, you may need to calculate the area of the antenna using the following formula:

Area = Reflector Length x Reflector Width

Once you have determined the area of the antenna, you can perform the power density near-field calculation.  The formula used to calculate the near-field estimation is:
