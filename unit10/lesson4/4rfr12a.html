Recommending Corrective Actions

After all information about the incident is analyzed, you should provide recommendations for corrective actions so incidents of this nature will be less likely to occur in the future.

Your primary responsibility when providing recommendations is to communicate to decision-makers any hazardous practices that contributed to the incident.  This includes any typical worker activities which may be safety hazards and could lead to future incidents.
