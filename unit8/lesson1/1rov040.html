Electromagnetic Spectrum

Wavelength and frequency are two more concepts important to the discussion of radiation.  Variations in wavelength and frequency produce different types of wave radiation and have different effects on the body.  These variations can best be seen using the electromagnetic spectrum, which can be viewed in three primary sections.
