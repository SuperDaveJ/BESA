Class 4 Lasers

Class 4 lasers are hazardous to the eyes and skin from direct beam exposures.  In addition, this type of lasers poses diffuse reflection and fire hazards.  Under certain conditions, Class 4 lasers can produce laser-generated air contaminants and hazardous plasma radiation.

Examples of Class 4 lasers include:

  • Tactical high energy laser.
  • Airborne lasers.
  • Precision guided munitions.
  • Laser weaponry.
  • Targeting and navigation.
