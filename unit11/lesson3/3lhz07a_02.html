Optical Density (OD) Requirements

The Optical Density (OD) is a unit-less number assigned during laser classification that describes the amount of energy that needs to be blocked in order to be protected from that laser.

The OD rating must be provided on the LEP and describes the amount of light energy the LEP lens blocks.  It’s important for the OD to be high enough to protect personnel from the laser while still allowing enough light through so work can be performed.

The OD for LEP is typically found on the ear piece or is etched on the front of the glasses.
