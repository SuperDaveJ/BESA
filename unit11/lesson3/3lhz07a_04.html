Eyewear Damage

When evaluating the proper use and maintenance of LEP, you should make sure the device does not have any scratches or damage to the lens.

If an absorptive LEP is scratched, it could still be functional. However, deep scratches reduce the amount of protection that may be provided by the LEP.

Reflective LEP such as those made from coated glass are not tolerant to scratches.

When the reflective coating from this type of LEP is removed or scratched, the glasses should not be used.
