Address Follow-Up Needs

As part of the OEHSA process, you should identify the need for follow-up sampling and develop a plan for reassessment, including sampling frequency.

If you are sampling as part of an established sampling strategy, frequency and follow-up needs should already be identified and should only change if circumstances affecting the potable water system change.  Reassessment frequency is determined by categorizing the risk associated with the area of concern (AOC) as high, medium, or low and may need to be reevaluated after all risks have been fully characterized.
