Communicate

Communication is a key component of the HRA process.  You must be proactive in communicating risks and recommendations to commanders about controlling those risks.  Initially, the commander will typically ask you for a recommendation or input; however, after a decision is made, commanders will likely assume that the recommendation remains valid unless they are told otherwise.  Therefore, it is important for you to provide on-going recommendations, updates, alternatives, and courses of action as needed based on routine and special surveillance.

You must also communicate health risks to personnel or community members whenever their health and safety are or may be affected.
