Sample Collection Methods

When defining your strategy, you will also choose a method for collecting samples. 

Both grab samples and composite samples can be collected manually or automatically.  Whichever technique is adopted, the success of the sampling strategy is directly related to the care exercised in the sample collection.  Optimum performance will be obtained by using trained personnel.

Along with determining whether to use manual or automatic sampling, you must determine what PPE may be required, the type of containers that may be used, and how much of the sample is required.

Selecting sampling methods for non-aqueous phase liquids (NAPLs) requires special considerations.
