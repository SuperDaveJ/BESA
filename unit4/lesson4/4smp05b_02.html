Automatic Sampling

Automatic samplers are being used increasingly because of their cost effectiveness, versatility, reliability, and capabilities.  Automatic samplers are available with widely varying levels of sophistication, performance, mechanical reliability, and cost.  No single automatic sampling device is ideally suited for all situations.  For each application, the following variables and factors should be considered in selecting an automatic sampler:

  • Variation of water or wastewater characteristics with time.
  • Variation of flow rate with time.
  • Specific gravity of liquid and concentrations of suspended solids.
  • Presence of floating materials.
  • The range of intended use.
  • The skill level required for installation of the automatic sampler.
  • The level of accuracy desired.
