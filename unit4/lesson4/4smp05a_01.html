Grab Samples

The most common type of sample used is the grab sample, which is an individual sample.  A grab sample characterizes the water quality at a particular time, and multiple grab samples taken at different times or locations can provide information on minimum and maximum concentrations of constituents.

Grab samples should be used when:

  • Parameters to be analyzed such as pH, dissolved oxygen (DO), and temperature are likely to change with storage.
  • Information on maximum, minimum, or variability of concentration of COCs is desired.
  • The history of water quality is to be determined.
