American Conference of Governmental Industrial Hygienists (ACGIH) Threshold Limit Values (TLVs)

These values, written by a private organization of professionals, represent recommended levels or guidelines for airborne concentrations.  The Air Force uses personnel contaminant exposure results and compares them to the standards set by ACGIH TLVs to make HRA decisions. The ACGIH TLVs can be found in the most recent Threshold Limit Values (TLVs®) for Chemical Substances and Physical Agents and Biological Exposure Indices (BEIs®) book published by ACGIH.
