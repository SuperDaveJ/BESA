Questions

Some questions you may ask when determining if air sampling is needed include:

  • Are there significant changes to the activity or process?
  • Is the variation in existing or historical data high?
  • Are sampling parameters well-defined and documented from previous evaluations?
  • Is there sufficient existing data?

The answer to all of these questions can affect how confident you are in the characterization. You would want to sample whenever you need more confidence and time or circumstances allow.
