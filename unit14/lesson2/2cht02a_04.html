Operational Limitations

The service life of the respirator cartridge may be affected by environmental conditions such as humidity that may cause the filter to clump.  The amount of time a worker can wear the RP may also be reduced by an increased level of effort required of the respirator wearer. For example, extreme physical exertion can cause a user to deplete the air supply in a self-contained breathing apparatus (SCBA) more quickly than in normal circumstances.
