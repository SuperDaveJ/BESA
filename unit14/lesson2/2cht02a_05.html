IDLH and/or O2 Deficient Conditions

Conditions in which contaminant levels are immediately dangerous to life or health (IDLH) or in which oxygen (O2) levels are less than 19.5% require special consideration when selecting respiratory protection.  Dire consequences could result if respiratory protection failed in either of these conditions and the worker could not escape quickly enough.  For these conditions, it is important to supply clean air to the workers with an SCBA.
