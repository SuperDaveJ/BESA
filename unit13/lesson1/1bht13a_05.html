Determine the Risk of Infections

When determining the risk of infections, consider the following questions:

  • Is the biological agent pathogenic?
  • Is there enough of the organism to live and reproduce?
  • Can the biological agent escape from the reservoir?
  • Can the organism be transferred through the environment by various means?
  • Is there a portal of entry into the new host?
  • Is the new host susceptible to the biological agent?
