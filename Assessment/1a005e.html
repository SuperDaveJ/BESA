Scenario Information

The sampling results from all four sites reveal the following information:

  • The results of the soil samples you collected at Site A reveal the presence of chemicals used for past maintenance and aircraft deicing.  The noise survey also indicates that the hazardous noise levels from the flightline and RFR levels are above the PEL.
  • At Site B, fuel contaminants and hydrocarbons were found in the soil that could be leaching into the groundwater.  Air sampling also indicates vaporization and volatilization of fuel contaminants near the subsurface fuel tanks.
  • Water sampling results from the stream near Site C indicate trace levels of trichloroethylene (TCE).  However, soil samples do not indicate the presence of any contaminants.
  • At Site D, soil contaminants from the former landfill are present, which could lead to leaching into the groundwater.  Air samples also indicate the presence of landfill gases, which can be explosive.

You will have to select a site for the water treatment facility, based on the results of your assessment, by determining a health risk estimate for the sites. To get a complete picture of the options before you determine the health risk estimate (HRE), you finish filling in the Conceptual Site Model (CSM) that you began earlier.
