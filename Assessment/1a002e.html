Scenario Information

After reviewing past HRAs, after action reports, and other environmental assessments conducted for surrounding areas, you learn the following information:

  • Winds are generally from the north between 10-15 mph.
  • The area near Site D was used for a landfill from 1943 until 1949.  The area was then flattened and several office buildings were placed there until the early 1970’s when foundation cracking caused the structures to be condemned and demolished.  After action reports concluded that foundation cracking was most likely due to the soil settling after being built on top of the landfill.
  • An area located near Site B in the annexed portion of land was used as a tanker field in the 1980’s, but the surface equipment has been removed. The area is currently an open field; however, several underground storage tanks were left at the site.
  • A paper mill is located just north of the base.
  • A contractor was hired to identify any water reservoirs on base.  The contractor’s report indicates two reservoirs, one deep and one shallow, within the base perimeter.

After gathering the information available about the four sites and the groundwater reservoirs, you request the initial grid breakout from CE to begin establishing a sampling plan for the sites.
