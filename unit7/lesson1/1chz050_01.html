Analyze a Confined Space

Use the following guidelines in your analysis of oxygen, flammability, and toxicity:

  • The oxygen content should be between 19.5% and 23.5%. 
  • Flammability must be less than or equal to 10% of the Lower Explosive Limit (LEL).
  • Identify toxicity by determining the occupational exposure limit (OEL), signs/symptoms, and target organs affected for chemicals present in the atmosphere of the space.
